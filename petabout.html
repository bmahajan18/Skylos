Who We Are



Welcome to Skylos Pet Adoption! We are a compassionate and dedicated team of animal lovers on a mission to find loving forever homes for our furry friends. With a shared passion for the well-being of animals, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of both pets and their future adopters.When you adopt from Tailwaggers, you become part of our extended family, and we're here to support you every step of the way on this beautiful journey of pet parenthood. Join us in creating a brighter future for these loving souls by adopting your perfect furry companion today.


Our Mission


This motto emphasizes the Skylos Pet Adoption organization's commitment to making a positive impact by finding forever homes for shelter animals, one adoption at a time.

Educate and Raise Awareness: Promote public awareness and education about breed discrimination.

Work with Tailwaggers Pet Adoption Organization: Collaborate with local animal welfare organizations, shelters, and rescue groups to address breed discrimination at a grassroots level.
